Whether you ordered flat-pack cabinets for a remodel or a quick add-on, these short walk-throughs show how each box snaps together. Watch once before you start and you'll save tape, trips, and tempers.

## How to Assemble: 30" Wide x 36" Tall Wall Cabinet

Our most common wall cabinet — perfect for the run above a 30" range. The video covers panel orientation, dado alignment, and the back-screw sequence that keeps everything square.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ys0XLHGYDh0

## How to Assemble: 30" Base Cabinet

The 30" base is the workhorse of most kitchens. This guide walks through the toe-kick, drawer-box prep, and getting the face frame flush before glue sets.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cEudIcrxGPY

## How to Assemble: 36" x 18" x 24" Deep Wall Cabinet

Deep wall cabinets — typically used over a fridge — need extra care so the back doesn't bow when you load them up. The video shows our recommended bracing pattern.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BdtkT-5ioOQ

## How to Assemble: 15" x 36" Tall Wall Cabinet

Narrow tall walls go together fast, but the door alignment is finicky if the box is even slightly racked. Watch the clamping sequence at 1:30 to keep it true.
