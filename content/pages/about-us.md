    body: |
      Welcome to Cabinets Plus, the premier destination for top-quality cabinets and countertops in Spokane, Washington. We are proud to introduce our team, lifelong buddies and business partners Art, Alex, and Eugene, who bring decades of experience in the construction industry to the table.

      ![](<https://cabinetsplus4630.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/uploads/pages/About Us/DSC02866.png>)

      Our journey began in 2014 when we realized that Spokane lacked a top-notch cabinets and countertops shop that was affordable for everyone. That's why we founded Cabinets Plus in 2015, right out of Eugene and Alex's garage, and opened our first showroom soon after. Our mission was simple: **to streamline the kitchen shopping experience and provide high-quality cabinets and countertops with top-notch design and installation services, all at a budget-friendly price.
      **

      At Cabinets Plus, we handle every aspect of your project in-house, from design to assembly, fabrication to installation. This approach has earned us the reputation of being a one-stop-shop, and we have grown into a 5,500 square foot showroom complete with dedicated shops for cabinet work and countertop fabrication.

      We take great pride in seeing a project through from start to finish and delivering a stunning and functional new addition to your kitchen or bathroom. Our team is committed to providing excellent quality, professional service, and unbeatable value every step of the way. Thank you for considering Cabinets Plus for your next project. We can't wait to work with you and help bring your dream kitchen or bathroom to life!
