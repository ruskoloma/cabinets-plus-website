slug: modern-transitional-kitchen-and-bath-cabinetry
description: >-
  This project features a cohesive mix of modern transitional cabinetry across the kitchen,
  bathrooms, pantry, and beverage area. Painted white and soft gray shaker cabinets are paired with
  warm stained wood accents, dark slab-style countertops, and stainless hardware. The kitchen
  centers on a large island with wood base cabinetry, farmhouse sink, tall uppers, and a deep blue
  tile backsplash. Bathrooms use light vanities, stone-look counters, large-format tile, and
  illuminated mirrors for a clean, tailored feel.
